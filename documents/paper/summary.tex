\section*{Summary of Changes}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item We thoroughly revised the introduction to highlight real-world applications of hub queries over meta-path induced relational graphs, as well as possible extension of our methods to more complex queries.
    \item We discussed existing works on applying KMV sketches to graph problems in the Related Work.
    \item We evaluated the approximation ratio $\epsilon$ achieved by our methods by varying the parameter $k$. The results have further verified that our methods can accurately answer Problems \ref{problem:approxHUB} and \ref{problem:approxPer}, achieving a small value of $\epsilon$ under the default parameter setting.
    \item We further conducted experiments to evaluate the impact of meta-paths on the performance of our methods. The results indicate that the efficiency of our methods is primarily determined by the number of edges in the corresponding matching graphs.
    \item We have moved some proofs from the original paper to the technical report~\cite{hud2023tr} due to space limitations.
\end{enumerate}
